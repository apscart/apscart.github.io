Get to Know Us

J. Todd Barrett R. Ph.

J. Todd Barrett R. Ph.


J. Todd Barrett R. Ph. is a co-founder of APS and serves as its President & Chief Executive Officer. Prior to the Company's formation, Mr. Barrett formed and operated Institutional Pharmacy, LLC, a long-term care pharmacy located in Richland, Mississippi. In 2001 Barrett formed and managed Covenant Pharmacy, LLC, a long-term care pharmacy located in Flowood, Mississippi serving the pharmaceutical needs of patients in nursing homes throughout Mississippi. Barrett is also a co-founder of Flowood, Mississippi based Transcript Pharmacy, a specialty pharmacy that provides medication therapy and pharmacy services to individuals with chronic conditions such as HIV/AIDS, hepatitis C as well as individuals who have received solid organ transplants. Mr. Barrett has served on the Mississippi Medicaid Pharmacy and Therapeutics Committee, and initiated several cooperatives and organizations including the Transplant Pharmacy Coalition, Specialty Pharmacies of America, the Mississippi Pharmacy Network, and Diabetes Supply, Inc. Barrett currently serves as a member of the Pharmacy Quality Alliance, Vice President of the Mississippi Pharmacist Association (MPhA) and as a member of the MHA Specialty.

Pharmacy Advisory Board. Barrett is a 1983 graduate from the University of Mississippi with a B.S. in chemistry and zoology. Following post-graduate study and research in chemistry Mr. Barrett began pharmacy school and graduated with a B.S. in 1988.
